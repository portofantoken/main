FC Porto Fan Token (PORTO)- Project Introduction

The FC Porto Fan Token (PORTO) is a native BEP-20 utility token designed to revolutionize the fan experience for all FC Porto supporters. FC Porto Fan Token (PORTO) options

Founded on 28 September 1893, FC Porto is a Portuguese professional sports club based in Porto. As one of the "Big Three" teams in Portugal, FC Porto is best known for their professional football team playing in the Primeira Liga, the top flight of Portuguese football. They are nicknamed Dragões (Dragons), for the mythical creature atop the club's crest, and Azuis e Brancos (Blue-and-whites), for the shirt colors.

FC Porto is one of the most decorated clubs in Portugal, with 79 major trophies, of which 72 were achieved in domestic competitions and 7 in international competitions. Estádio do Dragão, the home stadium of FC Porto, is a modern, functional and comfortable stadium with a capacity of 50,431 spectators - in line with the demands of high-level international football.
